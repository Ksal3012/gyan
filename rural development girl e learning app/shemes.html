Government Schemes

WHICH HELP FOR WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

1. Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA)

Launched by: Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)
Objective: PMGDISHA aims to provide digital literacy to rural areas, particularly to marginalized groups like rural women and girls.
Key Features: Training in digital literacy, outcome shows 53% beneficiaries are women, enabling access to e-services.

Learn More

2. Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK) Scheme

Launched by: Ministry of Women and Child Development
Objective: Empowers rural women through community engagement and skill-building.
Key Features: Capacity building, digital literacy, and community participation.

Learn More

3. Skill Development Programme for Rural Women by ICAR

Launched by: Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)
Objective: Enhance livelihoods through skill training in agriculture, crafts, and entrepreneurship.
Key Features: Fabric printing, eco-bag making, and poultry management.

Learn More

4. Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETI)

Launched by: Ministry of Rural Development
Objective: Promote entrepreneurship among rural youth through free training.
Key Features: Tailoring, food processing, and financial support for businesses.
